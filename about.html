Try these out:

  • Shrink the browser window - the UI should resize
  • Click buttons and things; they have fancy styling
  • Hover over intraboxes; fancy (JavaScript-free) animations be thar
  • Look at the clock in the small amount of time before the onload event happens; are we allowed to make wishes on that?
  • Do not take the previous bullet too seriously (especially the easter-egg-hating former admins)

Yes, I know radio buttons and check boxes do not work in all browsers. I will fix them at some point.

Browsers:

  • For best results, use Chrome.
  • It should work in Safari, Opera, and Firefox too, but Chrome is better.
  • It probably partially might work in Internet Explorer 10. In fact, some of it might even work in Internet Explorer 9. It has not been tested yet in either. Use Chrome.
  • It has been tested in older Gecko (SeaMonkey, old FireFox). It works somewhat, but it does not look as nice. Update your browser if it is not pretty. Or use Chrome. (Obviously the real thing will work in all browsers.)
  • I like Chrome :)
  • Feel free to substitue “Chromium” for “Chrome” in these bullets. Feel free to substitute “Chrome” or “Chromium” for your non-Chrom* browser if you use a non-Chrom* browser.

  • Oh, also, some of these will try to remember your theme. I only made it work fully in...well, you can probably guess which browser by now.
